## 1\. Product Vision & Objectives

**Vision:**
Empower Kurdish music lovers and musicians by providing the best online platform for discovering, learning, and playing Kurdish song chords, with a modern, intuitive, and community-driven experience.

**Objectives:**
*   Deliver a visually appealing, mobile-friendly, and easy-to-use chord website.
*   Offer unique features like chord transposition, interactive diagrams, and playback.
*   Monetize through freemium, subscriptions, and premium content.
*   Foster community engagement and user-generated content.
* * *
## 2\. Target Users & Personas

*   **Beginner Musicians:** Want easy access to chords and learning tools.
*   **Intermediate/Advanced Players:** Seek advanced features (transpose, downloads, recommendations).
*   **Kurdish Music Fans:** Interested in exploring and sharing Kurdish songs.
*   **Contributors:** Musicians who want to submit or correct chords.
* * *
## 3\. Functional Requirements

### A. Core Features

*   **Homepage:**
    *   Hero section with branding, search, and call-to-action.
    *   Featured songs and artists.
    *   Navigation: Songs, Artists, Tutorials, Community.
*   **Song Page:**
    *   Song title, artist, and audio playback.
    *   Lyrics with aligned chords.
    *   Chord transposition (up/down).
    *   Interactive chord diagrams (on click).
    *   Autoscroll for lyrics/chords.
    *   Download PDF (premium).
    *   Premium subscription banner and non-intrusive ad area.
*   **Artist/Genre Pages:**
    *   Artist bio, image, and song list.
    *   Genre pages with real images/icons.
    *   Filters for genre, popularity, and newness.
*   **Community Features:**
    *   Comments and ratings on songs.
    *   Favorites/playlists for registered users.
    *   User submissions/corrections.
*   **Mobile Responsiveness:**
    *   Fully responsive design.
    *   Large touch targets for navigation and chords.

### B. Monetization

*   **Freemium Model:**
    *   Free access to basic chords and features.
    *   Premium: unlimited transpositions, PDF downloads, ad-free, early access.
*   **Subscription Tiers:**
    *   Monthly/annual plans.
    *   Student/group discounts.
*   **Ads:**
    *   Non-intrusive ads for free users.
* * *
## 4\. Non-Functional Requirements

*   **Performance:** Fast load times, smooth interactions.
*   **Security:** Secure user data and payments.
*   **Scalability:** Support growing user base and content.
*   **Localization:** Kurdish and English support.
